Cheeseburger

Homepage

Description

A cheeseburger is a classic American sandwich made with a juicy grilled beef patty topped with melted cheese, usually served in a soft bun with lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, and condiments like ketchup, mustard, or mayonnaise. It’s a savory, satisfying meal that’s popular worldwide for its rich flavor and customizable toppings.

Ingredients (for 4 cheeseburgers)

  • 500g ground beef
  • Salt and black pepper
  • 4 slices of cheddar cheese
  • 4 burger buns
  • 1 tomato, sliced
  • 1 red onion, thinly sliced
  • Pickles (optional)
  • Ketchup, mustard, and/or mayonnaise
  • Butter or oil for toasting buns

Steps

  1. Form the patties
    1. Divide the ground beef into 4 equal portions and gently form into patties.
    2. Press a small dimple in the center of each to prevent puffing during cooking.
    3. Season both sides with salt and pepper.
  2. Cook the patties
    1. Heat a skillet, grill, or griddle over medium-high heat.
    2. Cook patties for about 3–4 minutes per side for medium doneness.
    3. During the last minute of cooking, place a slice of cheese on each patty and cover briefly to melt.
  3. Toast the buns
    1. Lightly butter the inside of each bun and toast on the skillet or grill until golden.
  4. Assemble the Burgers
    1. Place the lettuce on the bottom bun, followed by the patty with melted cheese.
    2. Add tomato, onion, pickles, and condiments to taste.
    3. Cover with the top bun and serve immediately.
