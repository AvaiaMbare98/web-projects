Lasagna

Homepage

Description

Lasagna is a beloved Italian dish known for its rich, layered composition and comforting flavors. It typically consists of wide, flat pasta sheets stacked with generous layers of savory meat sauce or a vegetable-based filling, creamy béchamel sauce or ricotta cheese, and a topping of melted mozzarella and grated Parmesan. Each layer is carefully assembled to create a hearty and satisfying meal that is baked until golden brown and bubbling. Lasagna is often enjoyed as a main course at family gatherings, holidays, and special occasions, offering a delicious combination of textures and flavors in every bite.

Ingredients

  • Meat sauce
    • 2 tablespoons olive oil
    • 1 onion, finely chopped
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • 500g ground beef or a mix of beef and pork
    • 800g canned crushed tomatoes
    • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
    • 1 teaspoon dried basil
    • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Béchamel Sauce
    • 4 tablespoons butter
    • 4 tablespoons all-purpose flour
    • 3 cups milk, warm
    • Salt and pepper to taste
    • A pinch of nutmeg (optional)
  • 9-12 lasagna noodles (pre-cooked or oven-ready)
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil (optional)

Steps

  1. Make the Meat Sauce
    1. Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add chopped onion and sauté until soft.
    2. Add garlic and cook for 1 more minute.
    3. Add ground beef, cook until browned. Drain excess fat if needed.
    4. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, basil, salt, pepper, and sugar.
    5. Simmer uncovered on low heat for 20–30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  2. Make the Béchamel Sauce
    1. Melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat.
    2. Whisk in flour and cook for 1–2 minutes (do not brown).
    3. Slowly add warm milk, whisking constantly to avoid lumps.
    4. Cook until the sauce thickens, about 5–7 minutes. Season with salt, pepper, and nutmeg.
  3. Preheat your oven to 190°C
  4. Assemble the Lasagna
    1. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce in the bottom of a baking dish.
    2. Add a layer of lasagna noodles.
    3. Spread a layer of meat sauce, then béchamel, and dot with ricotta if using.
    4. Repeat the layers (noodles → sauce → béchamel → cheese) until all ingredients are used.
    5. Finish with a top layer of noodles, sauce, béchamel, mozzarella, and Parmesan.
  5. Bake
    1. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes.
    2. Remove foil and bake for an additional 15–20 minutes, until top is golden and bubbling.
    3. Let rest for 10–15 minutes before serving.
